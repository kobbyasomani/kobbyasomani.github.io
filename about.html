About me

My interests and hobbies outside of web development include drawing, reading, music, video games, and photography.

My Interests & Hobbies

Reading

I've been a lover of books since a young age, and enjoy a wide range of reads including fantasy and science fiction, literary novels, and humour.

Music

I enjoy listening to and playing music when I have the chance, having played cello all through school and taken up the guitar as a teenager.

Art & Photography

I love drawing and painting, both digital and traditional, and have tried to maintain a sketcbook every year for the past 10 years or so. I've recently taken up photography.

Gaming

Gaming is another passion of mine, though I have much less time to do it now! I enjoy adventure games with rich and detailed worlds to explore.
